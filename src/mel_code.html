Set Out By 6am

Put your recycling out by 6 a.m. on your collection day. If you put it out later, it may not be picked up.

Don't put your recycling out too early. Setting it out before 8 p.m. the night before may result in a fine.

Make sure your recycling is easy to collect. If frozen or filled with water, it cannot be taken.
